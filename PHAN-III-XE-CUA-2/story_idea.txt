TITLE: The Twelve Trials of LANA

WORLD:
Ancient mythic empire inspired by Silk Road culture, desert kingdoms, celestial beasts, forgotten gods, cursed temples, and dangerous enchanted landscapes. 
Magic is real but forbidden. Prophecies echo through ruins and ancient scrolls.

MAIN CHARACTERS:
- LANA: A young empress-warrior chosen by the moon goddess. Symbol of beauty, grace, and ferocious will. Carries the Moonblade — a sacred weapon of pure light.
- ADAI: Her loyal guardian, stoic but deeply emotional.
- ASUKA: A priestess-scholar with forbidden knowledge of ancient curses.

THE PROPHECY:
LANA must complete **12 mythic trials** to save her empire from the “Shadow Eclipse”—a catastrophe that will drown her world in eternal darkness.

THE TWELVE TRIALS:
(Do NOT list as scenes; they are only high-level concepts for chapters.)

1. **The Guardian of Smoke Peaks**  
   LANA must defeat the obsidian bull of Mount Araz.

2. **The River of Whispering Souls**  
   LANA crosses a cursed river where the dead speak.

3. **The Labyrinth of Mirrors**  
   She faces illusions of her past and alternate selves.

4. **The Sand Titan of Kaijura Desert**  
   A colossal titan formed from storm and sand.

5. **The Forbidden Library Beneath the Palace**  
   LANA must retrieve a page from a living book.

6. **The Wolf of the Crimson Moon**  
   A sacred beast testing LANA’s honor, not strength.

7. **The Frozen Lake of Echoes**  
   A battle against memories that take physical form.

8. **The Mountain of Seven Winds**  
   Climb and survive attacks from wind-spirits.

9. **The Shrine of the Fallen Empress**  
   A spiritual trial that questions LANA’s destiny.

10. **The Black Sun Eclipse Collision**  
   A cosmic puzzle that must be solved before eclipse alignment.

11. **The Shadow Twin**  
   LANA must confront her own corrupted double.

12. **The Gate of Rebirth**  
   Final confrontation against the Eclipse God.

STRUCTURE (3-ACT):
ACT 1 — The Calling  
The empire faces omens of the Shadow Eclipse. LANA learns she is the chosen one. She begins the first trials.

ACT 2 — The Struggle  
The trials intensify. Betrayals. Loss. LANA's powers awaken. She confronts her past, fears, and identity.

ACT 3 — The Rebirth  
The final trials lead LANA to the Gate of Rebirth. She fights the Eclipse God and restores celestial balance.

PREFERENCES:
- Style: Mythic fantasy, epic tone, cinematic.
- Desired length: 12 chapters (each aligned with 1 trial).
- Additional note: Keep all text in English. Do NOT invent new named characters outside LANA, ADAI, ASUKA. Secondary characters may appear without names (monks, guards, merchants, beasts, spirits…).
